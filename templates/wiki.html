Coffee Chat

A coffee chat is an informal meeting with colleagues or mentors over coffee (or any beverage). It's an opportunity to network, discuss career goals, seek advice, and build professional relationships.

Brag Sheet

A brag sheet is a document where you keep track of your achievements, accomplishments, and contributions during your internship. It helps you remember your successes and can be valuable during performance evaluations or when seeking a return offer.

Midpoint Review

A midpoint review is a formal evaluation that typically occurs halfway through your internship. It's a chance to receive feedback from your supervisors, discuss your progress, and identify areas for improvement.

Final Review

The final review is the last evaluation you'll receive at the end of your internship. It assesses your overall performance and can influence whether you receive a return offer or a recommendation for future opportunities.

Important Tips

  • Always be proactive and take initiative in your work.
  • Seek feedback regularly to improve your performance.
  • Network with colleagues and attend company events.
  • Maintain a positive and professional attitude.
  • Ask questions and show curiosity to learn more.
  • Meet deadlines and communicate proactively if you encounter challenges.
  • Keep a record of your accomplishments using a brag sheet.
  • Respect company policies and maintain confidentiality.
  • Express gratitude to those who help and support you during your internship.
